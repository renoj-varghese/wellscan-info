What if food banks could better know the quality of their inventory?

In collaboration with UConn's Rudd Center for Food Policy and Obesity, and with support from Partnership For Healthier America, Feeding America, and others, WellSCAN is a suite of web/mobile applications made for food banks, food pantries, and other organizations to accomplish ranking the nutritional quality of their inventory.

This application is housed, licensed and serviced at the University of Connecticut, and includes the WellScan Global nutrition database, WellScan Connect API, WellScan Mini web-based/handheld scanning app.
